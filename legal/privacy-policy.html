Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data persolist ("the App") collects, how it's used, and your choices. We've kept this matched to what the App actually does — no boilerplate that doesn't apply to us.

Summary

  • You don't need to create an account or give us your name, email, or phone number to use persolist.
  • Your quiz answers and results are stored locally on your device.
  • To generate the written explanation of your result, your answers and computed scores (not your name or any personal identifier) are sent to a third-party AI service for processing.
  • We don't show ads, and we don't sell your data — we don't have any data to sell.

What we collect

Information you provide:

  • Your answers to the personality quiz questions.
  • Optional confidence ratings ("very me / somewhat me / rarely me") attached to your answers.

Information generated by the App:

  • Computed personality trait scores and the written explanation produced from your answers.
  • App settings (theme, sound preference).

Information we do not collect: your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or any device identifiers — unless a future version of the App explicitly asks for them, in which case this policy will be updated first.

How your data is used and where it goes

Stored on your device. Your quiz answers, results, and settings are stored locally on your device (via the Capacitor Preferences API). This data stays on your device and isn't automatically uploaded to any server we control.

Sent to a third-party AI provider. To generate the written explanation of your result, your computed trait scores and selected answer text are sent over an encrypted connection to NVIDIA's hosted meta/llama-3.1-8b-instruct model for processing. This request does not include your name or any other personal identifier — only your quiz responses and scores. NVIDIA handles this data under its own privacy practices, which are outside our control; we recommend reviewing NVIDIA's privacy policy for more detail.

Update checks. The App periodically checks for new versions via GitHub's public release API. This is a standard network request and doesn't include any of your personal data — though, like any internet request, your device's IP address is visible to whichever server handles that check, the same as visiting any website.

Your choices and rights

  • Delete your data: Since everything is stored locally, clearing the App's storage (Android Settings → Apps → persolist → Storage → Clear Data) or uninstalling the App permanently deletes everything persolist has stored about you on your device.
  • Opt out of AI analysis: Use Quick mode (8 seed questions, no AI calls) or enable Local-only mode in Settings — both options prevent your answers from being sent to NVIDIA.

Children's privacy

persolist is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children under this age. If you believe a child has used the App and provided information that should be removed, contact us at amardeepku0733@gmail.com and we'll address it.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for any data sent off your device. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we don't store sensitive personal data on any server we control, which limits what could ever be exposed.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App changes. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact us at amardeepku0733@gmail.com.
