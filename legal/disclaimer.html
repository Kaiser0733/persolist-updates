Not a clinical or diagnostic tool

persolist is not a psychological, psychiatric, or medical assessment. It is not created, reviewed, or endorsed by licensed mental health professionals, and it cannot diagnose any mental health condition, personality disorder, or any other clinical condition — in you or in anyone else.

The questions and scoring loosely borrow language from clinical personality psychology, including terms like "Cluster A / B / C" and "Dark Triad" traits (narcissistic, Machiavellian, and psychopathic tendencies). These terms describe ordinary, everyday behavioral leanings that most people have to some degree under certain conditions — stress, conflict, competition, and so on. A score on any of these scales is not evidence of a personality disorder, and is not a diagnosis of narcissism, psychopathy, or any other condition.

Please don't use this to diagnose yourself or other people

Don't use your result, or someone else's, to:

  • label yourself or another person with a clinical diagnosis,
  • make decisions about someone's character in a relationship, workplace, or legal dispute,
  • justify starting, escalating, or ending a conflict with someone.

If something in your result feels heavier than a personality quirk, or you're worried about your own mental health or someone else's, please talk to a licensed therapist, counselor, or doctor. This app isn't built or equipped to tell the difference between a personality pattern and something that needs real support.

How your result is generated

Your result comes from two things:

  1. A scoring formula applied to your answers, and
  2. An AI-generated written explanation based on that scoring.

AI-generated text can occasionally be imprecise, generic, or just wrong. Treat the written explanation as a conversation starter about yourself, not a fact.

If you're struggling

If you're in emotional distress, having thoughts of self-harm, or in crisis, please don't rely on this app for support — reach out to a crisis helpline, a trusted person, or emergency services in your country right away.

In India, you can contact iCall at 9152987821 or Vandrevala Foundation at 1860-2662-345. In the US, call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. In the UK, call 116 123 for Samaritans.
